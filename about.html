About Tech Innovators Club

We inspire students to create, learn, and lead through collaborative tech projects and events.

Who we are

Founded in 2017, Tech Innovators Club brings together students from diverse disciplines to solve real problems using technology. We combine workshops, hands-on projects, mentoring and competitions to craft a learning-first environment.

Our mission

To empower students with practical skills, mentorship and a collaborative community that encourages experimentation and leadership.
