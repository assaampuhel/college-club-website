About the Innovators Club

Established in 2018, the Innovators Club provides a platform for students to explore technology and creativity beyond academics. Our members come from diverse backgrounds including Computer Science, Electronics, and Mechanical Engineering, united by a shared passion for innovation.

We organize interactive sessions, coding challenges, and community projects to inspire students to create meaningful solutions for modern-day problems.
