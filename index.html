About Us

The Innovators Club is a student-led organization dedicated to nurturing creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving. We host hackathons, workshops, and tech events to help students explore the latest innovations in AI, Robotics, and Software Engineering.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world experience by encouraging collaboration, innovation, and leadership.
